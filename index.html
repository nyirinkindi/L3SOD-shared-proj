Cook Like a Chef: Meat Recipes

Recipe 1: Grilled Steak

Ingredients:

  • 2 steaks (sirloin or ribeye)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (optional)

Steps:

  1. Season the steaks with salt, pepper, and garlic.
  2. Preheat the grill to high heat.
  3. Grill steaks for 4-5 minutes each side for medium rare.
  4. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Recipe 2: Roast Beef

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg beef roast
  • Salt, pepper, herbs
  • Olive oil
  • Vegetables (carrots, potatoes)

Steps:

  1. Season the beef with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  2. Preheat oven to 200°C (392°F).
  3. Roast beef in the oven for about 20 minutes per pound.
  4. Let rest before slicing.
