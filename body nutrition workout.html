Body Nutrition

Healthy Meals

Balanced meals with proteins, carbs, and healthy fats are essential for muscle growth and recovery. Examples include:

  • Grilled chicken with quinoa and vegetables
  • Oatmeal with fruits and nuts
  • Salmon with sweet potato and green beans

Hydration

Staying hydrated improves performance and recovery. Drink plenty of water before, during, and after workouts. You can also include:

  • Coconut water
  • Electrolyte drinks
  • Green tea in moderation

Supplements

Supplements can support your nutrition plan but should complement a healthy diet:

  • Whey protein for muscle repair
  • Creatine for strength and endurance
  • Multivitamins for overall health
