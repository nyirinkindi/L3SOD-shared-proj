Collaborative Gym Areas

Cardio Zone

This area is equipped with treadmills, ellipticals, and exercise bikes, where members can work out together or join group cardio sessions.

Weight Training Zone

Featuring free weights, dumbbells, and weight machines, this zone encourages collaborative training and spotting exercises with friends.

Functional Training Zone

Open space for resistance bands, medicine balls, and bodyweight exercises. Members can train in groups, share tips, and motivate each other.

Yoga & Stretching Zone

A calm area with mats and props for yoga, pilates, and stretching exercises. Perfect for collaborative classes or partner stretches.
