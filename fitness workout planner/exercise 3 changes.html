Warm-up Routine

Before starting your workout, it's important to do a warm-up to get your body ready, boost blood flow, and prevent injuries. Try including these exercises in your warm-up:

  • Jumping Jacks: A simple full-body movement to raise your heart rate and loosen muscles.
  • Arm Circles: Circle your arms both small and large to warm up shoulders.
  • Leg Swings: Swing each leg forward/backward and side-to-side to loosen hips.
  • High Knees: Jog in place, lifting knees high to activate core and legs.
  • Butt Kicks: Jog while kicking heels toward your glutes to stretch quads.
  • Dynamic Lunges: Step forward into a lunge, return, and switch legs to warm lower body.
